What is Arknights?

An innovative tower defense game that offers strong strategic gameplay alongside a unique character roster, that are all conveyed in a beautiful art-style.

Injecting Life into Tower Defense

In Arknights, you command operators that act as “towers” that are strategically deployed across maps to dispatch foes.

A Roster That Just Works

As a gacha game, Arknights also takes pride in providing a wide variety of collectible characters, that are categorized into various roles and classes.

The Most Free to Play Friendly Title

While higher rarity operators may perform better, the game balance takes pride in the ability of its lowest rarity operators always being viable, no matter how difficult a stage may be.
